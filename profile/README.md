# D&D Mapp

> *Roll for initiative.* 🎲

D&D Mapp is a platform for Dungeon Masters and Players to manage every aspect of their Dungeons & Dragons fifth Edition experience — from character sheets and spellbooks to full Virtual Tabletop sessions with dynamic lighting and fog of war.

**Currently, in active development.**

## What you can do

- **Build and manage Player Characters** — track stats, spells, inventory, and more
- **Look up game content** — spells, items, creatures, backgrounds, and mechanics at your fingertips
- **Join Campaigns** — play alongside other Players and DMs on a shared platform
- **Run sessions on the Virtual Tabletop (VTT)** — battle simulation, dynamic lighting, and fog of war
- **Keep session notes** — private, shared with your whole party, or with specific members
- **DMs: build encounters and NPCs** — custom creature stats and encounter tools
- **DMs: craft your world** — write lore, build world and location maps, and pin lore to locations
- **Roll virtual dice** — right on the platform during sessions
- **Let the rules work for you** — a customizable game engine automatically enforces 5e rules so you can focus on the story

# Support

## Found a bug?

Open an issue using the **Bug** template. Include steps to reproduce, expected behavior, actual behavior, and your environment.

## Have a question?

- **Quick question or real-time chat** — join the [Discord server](https://discord.com/B9m6usp4qx)
- **Async question or something worth a permanent answer** — post in [Discussions → Q&A](https://github.com/orgs/dnd-mapp/discussions/categories/q-a)

## Have an idea?

Post in [Discussions → Ideas](https://github.com/orgs/dnd-mapp/discussions/categories/ideas). Ideas are reviewed at the maintainer's discretion and may be converted into planned work.
